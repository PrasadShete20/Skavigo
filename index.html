Our Story

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a comprehensive logistics powerhouse.

Formation of Rajita Clearing Agencies Pvt Ltd (1990)

Established as a pioneer in customs clearance, transportation, and warehousing services. Built a strong foundation of trust and long-term partnerships with clients across diverse industries.

Expansion of Services (2000–2010)

Diversified operations to cater to technology, manufacturing, agriculture, and FMCG sectors. Gained a reputation for reliability and excellence in supply chain management.

Strategic Partnerships (2010–2020)

Collaborated with global forwarding agents to provide comprehensive logistics solutions. Invested in technology and infrastructure to improve operational efficiency.

Formation of Skavigo Logistics (2024)

Launched as a sibling company to Rajita Clearing Agencies Pvt Ltd, leveraging over 30 years of expertise. Established a seamless "one-roof solution" for integrated logistics services.

Vision Forward (2024 Onwards)

Aspiring to lead the logistics sector with cost-effective, efficient, and reliable solutions. Actively exploring partnerships with global forwarder networks to expand reach.
