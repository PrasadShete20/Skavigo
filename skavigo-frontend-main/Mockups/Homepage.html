Who We Are

Our Journey

Formation of Rajita Clearing Pvt Ltd (1990)

Established as a pioneer in customs clearance, transportation, and warehousing services. Specialized in providing end-to-end logistics solutions for diverse industries. Built a strong foundation of trust and long-term partnerships with clients.

Expansion of Services (2000–2010)

Diversified operations to cater to sectors such as technology, manufacturing, agriculture, and FMCG. Gained a reputation for reliability and excellence in logistics and supply chain management. Strengthened relationships with global logistics partners and agents.

Strategic Partnerships (2010–2020)

Collaborated with forwarding agents to provide comprehensive logistics solutions. Played a pivotal role in managing customs clearance, transportation, and warehousing for major industries. Invested in technology and infrastructure to improve operational efficiency and client satisfaction.

Formation of Skavigo Logistics Pvt Ltd (2024)

Launched as a sibling company to Rajita Clearing Pvt Ltd, leveraging over 30 years of industry expertise. Designed to provide integrated forwarding, customs clearance, transportation, and warehousing services. Established a seamless "one-roof solution" to enhance client experience and operational efficiency.

Vision and Mission of Skavigo Logistics Pvt Ltd (2024 Onwards)

Aspire to lead the logistics and supply chain sector by offering cost-effective, efficient, and reliable solutions. Actively exploring partnerships with global forwarder networks to expand services and reach. Committed to leveraging expertise to exceed client expectations and build enduring partnerships.
